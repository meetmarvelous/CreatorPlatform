
Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Creator Subscription Platform

1. Product Overview
The product is a web platform that allows content creators to publish exclusive content and earn money from their audience through subscriptions, one time purchases, and tips. The platform supports many creator categories such as education, fitness, entertainment, music, lifestyle, and premium communities.

2. Objectives
Business Goals
- Build a scalable creator economy platform
- Enable creators to earn recurring income
- Allow fans to support and interact with creators
- Generate revenue through platform commission

User Goals
Creators want a simple way to monetize content and manage subscribers.
Fans want easy access to exclusive content and secure payments.

3. Target Users
Creators: coaches, musicians, influencers, podcasters, teachers, analysts, fitness trainers, adult creators.
Fans: users who pay to access creator content.

4. Core Features
User Accounts, Creator Profiles, Content Posting, Subscription System, Direct Purchases, Tips, Messaging, Notifications.

5. Payments
Secure payment processing for subscriptions, purchases, and tips. Platform takes a percentage fee.

6. Creator Dashboard
Earnings analytics, subscriber management, content management, withdrawals.

7. Admin Panel
User management, moderation, payment monitoring, analytics.

8. Security
Encrypted passwords, secure payments, abuse prevention, protected content.

9. MVP Scope
Accounts, creator pages, content posting, subscriptions, payments, basic admin tools.

10. Future Features
Mobile apps, livestreaming, referrals, AI moderation, recommendations.

Why Crypto Is Attractive

Adult platforms like:
	•	OnlyFans
	•	Fansly

still rely heavily on traditional payment processors (Visa/Mastercard). That creates:
	•	Chargeback risks
	•	Frozen payouts
	•	Regional restrictions
	•	Account shutdown threats

Crypto reduces:
	•	Chargebacks (transactions are final)
	•	Card network control
	•	Geographic limits

That’s a big competitive angle.

⸻

🚨 But Here’s the Real Risk

Crypto alone is NOT enough.

You must consider:

1️⃣ Volatility

If a creator earns $500 today and token drops 15% tomorrow, they’re angry.

Solution:
Use stablecoins only (like USDT / USDC).

⸻

2️⃣ User Friction

Most fans don’t know how to:
	•	Open wallets
	•	Buy crypto
	•	Send transactions

If it’s too complicated, they won’t subscribe.

So you need:
	•	Very simple payment UX
	•	Possibly a fiat → crypto gateway

⸻

3️⃣ Legal & Compliance

Even crypto platforms must:
	•	Verify age
	•	Prevent illegal content
	•	Follow AML rules in many jurisdictions

Crypto doesn’t remove legal responsibility.
