# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Creator Subscription Platform

## 1. Product Overview

The product is a web platform that allows content creators to publish exclusive content and earn money from their audience through subscriptions, one time purchases, and tips. The platform is not limited to adult content. It supports many creator categories such as education, fitness, entertainment, music, lifestyle, and premium communities.

Fans can subscribe to their favorite creators and gain access to exclusive posts, videos, files, and live sessions.

The goal is to build a platform where creators can monetize their audience directly without relying on advertising revenue.

---

# 2. Objectives

### Business Goals

• Build a scalable creator economy platform
• Enable creators to earn recurring income
• Allow fans to support and interact with creators
• Generate revenue through platform commission

### User Goals

Creators want:
• A simple way to monetize their content
• Control over pricing and audience
• Tools to manage their subscribers

Fans want:
• Easy access to exclusive content
• Secure payments
• Direct interaction with creators

---

# 3. Target Users

### Creators

People who want to sell premium content online.

Examples:
• Coaches
• Musicians
• Influencers
• Podcasters
• Adult content creators
• Teachers
• Analysts
• Fitness trainers

### Fans / Subscribers

Users who pay to access content from creators.

---

# 4. Core Features

## 4.1 User Accounts

Users can register and log in as:

• Creator
• Fan

Account features:
• Email verification
• Password reset
• Profile management
• Secure authentication

---

# 4.2 Creator Profiles

Each creator has a public profile containing:

• Profile picture
• Bio / description
• Subscription price
• Preview posts
• Number of subscribers
• Social links

Creators can customize their page.

---

# 4.3 Content Posting

Creators can upload different content types:

• Videos
• Images
• Text posts
• Audio
• Files
• Livestream sessions

Posts can be:
• Free
• Paid
• Subscriber only

---

# 4.4 Subscription System

Creators can set a monthly subscription price.

Fans can:
• Subscribe
• Cancel subscription
• Renew subscription

The system manages:
• Billing cycles
• Access control
• Subscription history

---

# 4.5 Direct Purchase Content

Creators can sell individual content without requiring a subscription.

Examples:
• Premium videos
• Courses
• Tutorials
• Exclusive photos

---

# 4.6 Tips

Fans can send tips to creators.

This helps creators earn outside subscriptions.

---

# 4.7 Messaging

Fans can message creators.

Possible options:
• Free messages
• Paid messages
• Media attachments

---

# 4.8 Notifications

Users receive notifications for:

• New posts
• Messages
• Subscription renewal
• Creator announcements

---

# 5. Payments

The platform must support secure payments.

### Payment Features

• Subscription billing
• One time purchases
• Wallet balance
• Tips

### Payment Providers

• Crypto payments

### Revenue Model

Platform takes a percentage from creator earnings.

Example:
• Platform fee: 10 to 20 percent.

---

# 6. Creator Dashboard

Creators can manage their business through a dashboard.

Features:
• Earnings analytics
• Subscriber management
• Post management
• Withdrawal requests
• Pricing controls

---

# 7. Admin Panel

Platform administrators can manage the entire system.

Admin features:

• User management
• Creator approvals
• Content moderation
• Payment monitoring
• Dispute handling
• Platform analytics

---

# 8. Security Requirements

• Encrypted passwords
• Secure payment processing
• Anti piracy protections
• Content access protection
• Rate limiting and abuse detection

---

# 9. Moderation and Safety

The platform must allow:

• Content reporting
• Creator verification
• Category restrictions
• Automated moderation tools

Optional:
• Age verification for sensitive content
